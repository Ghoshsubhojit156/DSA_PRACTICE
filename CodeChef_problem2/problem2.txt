You are given an array of size n.
Your task is to rotate the array for K times.

The Second line will contain the number of times you need to rotate the array.
Note:

I will use JAVA language. You can choose any of the languages.

Don't need for the constraints just practice using bruteforce method.

Example needed to be solved:- 

Input
5
2
1 2 3 4 5
output:
4 5 1 2 3

Input
5
4
10 20 30 40 50
output:
20 30 40 50 10

Input
1
3
12
output
12
